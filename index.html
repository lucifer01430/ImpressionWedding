About Us


At Impressions Wedding and Events, we believe that every love story deserves a celebration as unique as the couple itself. With over a decade of experience in the wedding and event planning industry, our team is dedicated to turning your vision into reality. From intimate ceremonies to grand celebrations, we handle every detail with care, creativity, and a passion for perfection.

Our journey began with a simple mission: to create moments that last a lifetime. We pride ourselves on our personalized approach, ensuring that every event reflects your style, culture, and personality. Whether you're dreaming of a romantic garden wedding, a luxurious ballroom affair, or a destination celebration, we’re here to make it happen.

Our Services

Wedding Planning

We offer full-service wedding planning, taking care of every detail from venue selection to vendor coordination. Our team ensures that your big day runs smoothly, allowing you to focus on making memories with your loved ones.

Decor & Design

Transform your venue into a breathtaking space with our bespoke decor services. From floral arrangements to lighting and table settings, we create a cohesive design that matches your theme and vision.

Event Management

Our event management services ensure flawless execution on the day of your event. We coordinate timelines, manage vendors, and handle any last-minute challenges, so you can enjoy your celebration stress-free.

Destination Weddings

Dreaming of a wedding in a far-off location? We specialize in planning destination weddings, handling logistics, travel arrangements, and local vendor coordination to make your dream wedding a reality.

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should we book your services? +

We recommend booking at least 6-12 months in advance to secure your date, especially for peak wedding seasons. However, we can accommodate last-minute bookings depending on availability.

Do you handle destination weddings? +

Absolutely! We specialize in destination weddings and have planned events in locations like Goa, Udaipur, and even international destinations like Bali and Italy. We take care of all logistics, travel arrangements, and local vendor coordination.

Can you work within our budget? +

Yes, we work closely with our clients to create a wedding that fits their budget. We offer customizable packages and can suggest cost-effective solutions without compromising on quality.

What makes Impressions different from other planners? +

Our personalized approach, attention to detail, and passion for creating unique experiences set us apart. We treat every event as if it were our own, ensuring that every moment is perfect.
